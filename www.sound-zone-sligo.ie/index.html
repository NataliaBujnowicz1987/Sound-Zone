- ABOUT -

Sound Zone is a totally new place in this part of Ireland. Our goal is to promote jungle, dnb and breakbeat music, we've wanted to create positive and open place for everyone who wants to feel a different music experience. We based on London's music culture - the heart of electronic music.

Sound Zone is a music club, located in the old factory in Sligo - we have 2 main stages there - for bigger and smaller events. This place is open every weekend (from Friday to Sunday). We start early morning with workshops and diffrent music competitions - there are diffrent activities every weekend, check the events tab regularly.

The founders of Sound Zone are Buster Bloodvessel, a famous vocalist of the English ska band - Bad Manners, and a well-known drum and bass producer and one of the best dj's in the world - dj Hype. Sound Music exists in Sligo, Copenhague, London and Warsaw in Poland.
